Upma

Upma is one of the best south-indian dish and it is instant dish
Majority of south indian middle class families prefer Upma as their breakfast as it is very easy and fast and more over it is very cheap
Based on the ingrediants you can make it differently everytime

ingrediants required

Soaking rice and lentils
Making dosa batter in a blender or mixer-grinder
Fermenting dosa batter
